Research Impact Snapshot.

This section highlights research capability and analytical execution. Each case shows the business context, method rigor, strategic insight, recommendation, measurable impact, and tools.

Scope note: Dataset A (Da Nang segmentation) includes 74,896 reviews across 492 hotels. Dataset B (Vietnam PRCA) is a separate study with 97,014 reviews across approximately 1,000 properties.

Experience-Based Hotel Segmentation from Aspect-Level Sentiment Analysis

  • Business problem: Hotel teams relied on aggregate ratings and lacked a diagnostic view of which experience dimensions drove dissatisfaction by guest type.
  • Method used: Built an end-to-end ABSA pipeline with aspect-schema engineering, transformer sentiment scoring, confidence diagnostics, and robust clustering validation.
  • Outcome/impact metric: Produced a reusable 10-segment framework from 74,896 reviews across 492 hotels (bootstrap ARI = 0.94).
  • Tools: Python (Pandas), Sentence Transformers, RoBERTa, scikit-learn (GMM/K-means).

Student Nutrition & Diet Quality

  • Business problem: Universities lacked local evidence on which modifiable factors most strongly improve student diet quality.
  • Method used: Implemented validated survey instruments and multivariable linear modeling with demographic control variables.
  • Outcome/impact metric: Generated a 280-student evidence base and translated findings into practitioner-facing recommendations (FCBEM 2025).
  • Tools: SPSS, R, survey design, regression diagnostics, Excel.

Virtual Influencers & Gen Z Trust

  • Business problem: Brands needed robust evidence on whether virtual influencers can sustain trust-led conversion with Gen Z.
  • Method used: Built a theory-driven quantitative model and tested direct and mediated effects through regression-based analysis.
  • Outcome/impact metric: Converted findings into publication-grade outputs presented at EEEU Conference 2023.
  • Tools: Qualtrics, SPSS, hypothesis testing, mediation analysis.
