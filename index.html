Selected Work

Work that shows how I think.

Three projects that show the problem, method, result, and evidence in one view.

Hotel Customer Segmentation

  • Problem: Hotel teams needed more than aggregate ratings to see which experience issues drive dissatisfaction by guest segment.
  • Method: Built an aspect-based sentiment pipeline and validated clustering on 74,896 reviews across 458 hotels.
  • Result: Produced a reusable 10-segment framework for experience-led decision making.
  • Evidence: Self-reported internal project output with bootstrap ARI diagnostics and segment-level interpretation.

Nutrition Knowledge & Diet Quality

  • Problem: Universities lacked local evidence on what shapes student diet quality.
  • Method: Analyzed survey responses from 280 students with multivariable modeling and demographic controls.
  • Result: Identified modifiable drivers that informed phased intervention priorities.
  • Evidence: Conference-paper context in FCBEM 2025 with the output also listed on ORCID.

Virtual Influencers & Gen Z Trust

  • Problem: Brands needed evidence on how virtual influencers relate to trust and purchase intent with Gen Z.
  • Method: Tested direct and mediated relationships with a theory-led survey model.
  • Result: Framed credibility, relevance, and disclosure as the key trust levers.
  • Evidence: Springer chapter published online June 18, 2025 (DOI: 10.1007/978-981-96-4116-1_9).
