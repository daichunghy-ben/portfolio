Research Impact Snapshot.

This section highlights research capability and analytical execution. Each case summarizes the business context, method rigor, evidence status, decision-use interpretation, and tools.

Experience-Based Hotel Segmentation from Aspect-Level Sentiment Analysis

  • Business problem: Hotel teams relied on aggregate ratings and lacked a diagnostic view of which experience dimensions were associated with dissatisfaction by guest type.
  • Method used: Built an end-to-end ABSA pipeline with aspect-schema engineering, transformer sentiment scoring, confidence diagnostics, and robust clustering validation.
  • Outcome/impact metric: Reported (self-reported): a reusable 10-segment framework from 74,896 reviews across 458 hotels; bootstrap ARI diagnostic shown as 0.94 in internal project outputs.
  • Tools: Python (Pandas), Sentence Transformers, RoBERTa, scikit-learn (GMM/K-means).

Student Nutrition & Diet Quality

  • Business problem: Universities lacked local evidence on which modifiable factors are associated with student diet quality.
  • Method used: Used reported survey instruments and multivariable linear modeling with demographic control variables.
  • Outcome/impact metric: Reported (self-reported): 280-student evidence base translated into practitioner-facing recommendations (FCBEM 2025 context).
  • Tools: SPSS, R, survey design, regression diagnostics, Excel.

Virtual Influencers & Gen Z Trust

  • Business problem: Brands needed evidence on how virtual influencers relate to trust and purchase intent with Gen Z.
  • Method used: Built a theory-driven quantitative model and tested direct and mediated effects through regression-based analysis.
  • Outcome/impact metric: Reported: publication-context output in EEEU 2024 conference context; Springer first online: June 18, 2025 (DOI: 10.1007/978-981-96-4116-1_9).
  • Tools: Qualtrics, SPSS, hypothesis testing, mediation analysis.
