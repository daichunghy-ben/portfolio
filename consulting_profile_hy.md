## 8) Research and Consulting Project Portfolio
_10 principal applied projects + 1 companion framework in current portfolio scope._

### Hospitality and Tourism
1. Hotel Customer Segmentation from Aspect-Level Sentiment (Da Nang)
- Scope: 74,896 reviews across 458 hotels.
- Method: ABSA + K-Means (primary) with GMM robustness checks.
- Output: 10 traveler segments for service and positioning decisions.

2. Asymmetric Hotel Attribute Impacts (PRCA)
- Scope: 97,014 reviews from approximately 1,000 Vietnam properties.
- Method: Sentence-BERT mapping + OLS (HC1) + PRCA/Kano analysis.
- Output: Basic/performance/excitement attribute classification for strategy prioritization.

3. Hotel Value Framework (Companion Framework)
- Scope: Conceptual translation layer linked to segmentation stream.
- Method: NLP-to-value segmentation framing.
- Output: Value-led segment interpretation for decision support.

### Mobility and Policy
4. Motorbike Phase-Out Policy Acceptance
- Scope: Hanoi transition policy context.
- Method: Discrete choice policy sequencing translation.
- Output: Pull/support-first policy sequencing implications.

5. Consumer Preferences for EV Aesthetic Design and Technology
- Scope: n = 212 young consumers (Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City).
- Method: DCE with MNL, mixed logit, and 2-class latent class estimation.
- Output: Segment split (65% pragmatic / 35% aesthetic) and scenario trade-off guidance.

### Creator Economy and Social Commerce
6. Leveraging Influencer Retention on UGC Platforms
- Scope: n = 565 active UGC users in Vietnam.
- Method: Four-E SEM model (Experience, Expectation, Equity, Engagement -> Retention).
- Output: Explained 55% engagement variance and 70% retention variance (reported in portfolio context).

7. Virtual Influencers and Gen Z Trust
- Scope: Publication-linked trust pathway study context.
- Method: Quantitative modeling and trust-pathway synthesis.
- Output: Practical trust transfer and communication guardrails.

### Health and Consumer Behavior
