Project overview

Review text became a segmentation system.

We applied NLP and aspect-level sentiment analytics to thousands of guest reviews to uncover what truly drives satisfaction in Da Nang hotels.

Aspect-level understanding reveals distinct guest priorities, enabling hotels to tailor experiences that matter most.

  • Granular aspect extraction
  • Sentiment scoring at scale
  • Data-driven segmentation

The key challenge

Different guests judge the same stay differently.

Hotels face diverse expectations across experiences and budgets. Average ratings hide where each guest group is delighted, disappointed, or undecided.

How might unstructured review data become clear, actionable segments for strategy and resource allocation?
